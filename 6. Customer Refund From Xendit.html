Customer Refund From Xendit

Please follow the step below for processing customers application

Objective:
  • To oversee and manage the task related to the customer’s query regarding the refund process.

Assumptions:
  • The customer deposit was never validated successfully.

Concern info

  • Reference No.: __
  • Date of Payment: ______
  • Time of Payment: ______
  • Total Amount Paid: ____
  • Requested Amount: _____
  • Bank/Branch: __________
  • Reason:________________


STEPS


CDMG

  1. CDMG-CS receives a ticket from the Customer with Credit/Debit Refund concern and evaluates the issue.
  2. CDMG-CS gathers all important info and proof of payment from the customer and creates task, load Customer Refund From Xendit template and assign to B&C for validation.

Billing and Collection (Refund)

  1. B&C reviews the provided information.
If customer information is correct, B&C initiates the refund process.
    • Visit the link: https://docs.xendit.co/credit-cards/refunds
    • Login as administrator
    • Open the details page and click the “Refund” button in the top right corner.
    • After clicking Submit, enter your PIN. Once confirmed, the refund request is sent to the bank.
    • Download the refund receipt from the refund detail page for customer proof if needed.
    • If the customer received the successful refund email, proceed to Step 4. If the customer didn’t receive a successful refund email confirmation. Go back to Step 3.
If customer information is incorrect. Add note regarding the information to be updated then reply to the ticket in order to get the correct information from the customer. Afterwards, proceed to Step 3.
  1. B&C mark the task as done and assign the next step to CDMG for review and archiving

CDMG

  1. CDMG-CS set workflow status to: Done.Then review and archive the task.
  2. CDMG-CS updates and close the ticket.
