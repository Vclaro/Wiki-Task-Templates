Billing concern (Void payment process)

This process applies to any related concerns listed below

  • Error in payment process
  • No payment confirmation received
  • Pending status for a long time
  • Cannot proceed with payment process
  • Paid but not reflected in the customer portal
  • Change payment option

Objective:
  • The Customer would like validate if the payment was recieved or
  • The Customer would like to change payment option

Assumptions:
  • The customer paid an invoice or proforma invoice in the Customer portal
  • The invoice is in "pending" status
  • Dragonpay payment Reference Number has expired

Concern info

  • Payment channel: __
  • Deducted: (yes/no)
  • Proof of payment:
  • Reference Number: __
  • Payment deadline expired: yes/no
  • Concern summary: __

STEPS


CDMG

  1. CDMG-CS receives a ticket from the Customer with Billing concern and evaluate the issue.
  2. CDMG-CS gathers all important info from the customer and creates task, load Billing concern template and assign to B&C group if no deduction else assign to IT.

Billing and Collection (Void payment)

  • No deduction to Customer's balance. Proceed with the void payment process by clicking the "void payment" button. The system will update the invoice status to "Unpaid" and Dragonpay will tag the previous reference number to "Void". The "Pending" invoice will be updated to "Unpaid" status in the Customer Portal.
  • Advise the Customer that they can pay the same invoice in the Customer Portal.
  • |Process end here; Assign the next step to CDMG for review and archiving.

IT (Validate payment)

  • Contact Dragonpay support and request for payment revalidation, and provide the proof of payment. If confirmation was received, advise the B&C to check
  • |Process end here; Assign the next step to CDMG for review and archiving.

CDMG

  1. CDMG-CS set workflow status to: Done.Then review and archive the task.
  2. CDMG-CS updates and close the ticket.
